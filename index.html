Public service advertising is primarily designed to inform and educate rather than sell a product or service. Besides, it emphasizes on changing public opinion and raise public awareness on some important issues

Suggett, 2018

Vasiloaia (2009) indicates that “the language of advertising frequently uses figures of speech and other stylistic devices that are considered typical of poetic language, such as puns, metaphors, neologisms,alliteration, assonance or rhyme” to witness “the high degree of creativity involved in advertising language and contributes to the secondary function of advertising to entertain the recipients”

p. 295

The language of advertisements always be positive and emphasize on the superiority of their products. They use various linguistics techniques to catch attention, arouse desire, induce action and contribute towards satisfaction

    • Conclusion

    Linguistic features Chinese PSA slogans mostly adopted are

    rhyme, gradable adjectives in basic forms and superlatives, verb phrases in incomplete sentences, declarative sentences and the repetition.

    Linguistic features English PSA slogans mostly adopted are

    alliteration, gradable adjectives in basic forms, verb phrases in incomplete sentences, imperative sentences, parallelism and repetition.


    The new discovery of combination uses of sentence types

    Chinese PSA slogans have more combination use.

    The combination uses of sentence types are more effective than using a single type.

    The result of this study conforms to the theory of Vasiloaia’s (2009) and Fomukong’s (2016).
